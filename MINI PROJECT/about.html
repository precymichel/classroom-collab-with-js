What is Classroom Collaboration?

Classroom Collaboration is a platform designed to bring students and teachers together. It facilitates real-time interactions, provides collaborative tools, and supports assignments, discussions, and more!

Key Features:

  • Real-time discussion forums
  • Assignment submissions and grading
  • Notifications and messaging
